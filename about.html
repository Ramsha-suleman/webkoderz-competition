About Us

Welcome to the Tech Titan Club, a coding society of Ned University! We are a community of students who share a passion for coding, technology, and innovation. Our society was founded in 2020 with the goal of providing a platform for students to learn, grow, and connect with like-minded individuals.

Our society is open to all students of Ned University who are interested in coding, regardless of their level of experience. Whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting out, our society provides a welcoming and supportive environment where you can expand your knowledge and skills.

Join Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to create awareness amongst the youth and to emphasize the prominence of programming through workshops and seminars.

Moreover, we aim to allot funds and financial support to creative minds and help them to sharpen their artistry so that they can flourish their startups and become the innovators of tomorrow.

Join Us

Our Aim

As a student-led society Our Aim is to provide opportunities for our new members to develop leadership and teamwork skills.

At our society, we value creativity, innovation, and community. We believe that everyone has something to contribute and that we can all learn from each other. Our society is a space where members can exchange ideas, share knowledge, and work together to solve real-world problems using technology.
